BIO

Spider-Man is a 2002 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. Directed by Sam Raimi from a screenplay by David Koepp, it is the first installment in Raimi's Spider-Man trilogy and stars Willem Dafoe, Kirsten Dunst, James Franco, Cliff Robertson, and Rosemary Harris as the title character, along with Tobey Maguire. The movie chronicles Spider-Man's origins and early superhero career. After being bitten by a genetically modified spider, ostracized teenage genius Peter Parker develops spider-like superhuman abilities and takes on a masked identity to fight crime and injustice in New York City; He is faced with the evil Green Goblin in the process.
